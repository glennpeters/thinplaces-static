Thin Places Photography

Thin Places is a collection of photos by me, Glenn Peters.

My first camera was a wide format children's camera of some sort. I likely still have photos from that camera in a bin. When I was about six, my uncle came for a visit, and was taking pictures of my sister when I stepped in and started insisting the shot be framed in a certain way. When he finally got the photos developed, he was so impressed he sent them back with a letter about the story. Since then I've used all sorts of cameras and formats, trying to find the unexpected angle or moment.
